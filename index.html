About Mercy Tours & Travels

Based in the heart of Uganda, Mercy Tours & Travels is your gateway to experiencing the Pearl of Africa's incredible wildlife, rich culture, and breathtaking landscapes. With years of experience in sustainable tourism, we are committed to providing authentic adventures while supporting local communities and conservation efforts.

Our mission is to create unforgettable memories while promoting responsible tourism that benefits both our guests and the beautiful country of Uganda. From the misty mountains where mountain gorillas roam to the vast savannahs teeming with wildlife, we'll guide you through Uganda's most spectacular destinations.

Our Tours & Activities

Discover Uganda's incredible diversity through our carefully crafted tours and activities designed for every type of adventurer.

Safari Tours

Experience Uganda's Big Five and diverse wildlife in Queen Elizabeth, Murchison Falls, and Kidepo Valley National Parks.

Birdwatching

Discover over 1,000 bird species including the rare Shoebill Stork and various endemic species in Uganda's diverse habitats.

Nature Walks

Enjoy peaceful walks through Uganda's forests, wetlands, and botanical gardens while learning about local flora and fauna.

Gorilla Tracking

Once-in-a-lifetime encounters with mountain gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest and Mgahinga National Parks.

Chimpanzee Tracking

Track our closest relatives in Kibale Forest and Budongo Forest, home to Uganda's largest chimpanzee populations.

Cultural Tours

Immerse yourself in Uganda's rich cultural heritage through visits to local communities, traditional ceremonies, and craft workshops.

City Tours

Explore Kampala's vibrant markets, historical sites, religious centers, and experience the bustling energy of Uganda's capital.
